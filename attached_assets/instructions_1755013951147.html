How to Play

Controls

  • - Move
  • WASD - Move
  • - Jump
  • R - Restart Level
  • ESC - Pause

Objectives

  • Collect all stars in each level
  • Avoid enemies and obstacles
  • Reach the level exit
  • Complete levels with the highest score

Tips

  • Plan your jumps carefully
  • Look for hidden stars
  • Use the pause menu for strategy
  • Practice makes perfect!
